About Me

Anas Bouali (email) : I am a doctoral candidate at LMA (Avignon University), under the supervision of Prof. Térence Bayen (Avignon university) and Prof. Loïc Bourdin (Limoges university). I am currently working on Hybrid optimal control & Optimal control problems with control loss.

Research Interests

  • Pontryagin maximum principle
  • Nonpermanent controls, sampled-data controls
  • hybrid problems
  • Optimal control problems with control loss regions
  • Controllability of PDEs
  • Observability property
  • Interior controllability of the wave equation
  • Boundary controllability of the wave equation
